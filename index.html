Lex Orandi

A Journal of the Liturgical Movement

Mission. Lex Orandi is a multilingual, open-access, double-blind peer reviewed academic journal committed to furthering liturgical renewal in a complex and evolving world. Pan-ecclesial, pan-methodological, and public-facing, Lex Orandi makes excellent liturgical scholarship accessible to all in order to contribute to the good of worshipping communities worldwide and to help advance the cause of church unity.

Who We Are. Lex Orandi was founded as a conscious attempt to further the precious heritage of its two predecessor journals, Questions Liturgiques and Worship. Inspired by the Belgian and American liturgical movements, Lex Orandi preserves their defining characteristics: a strong focus on methodological experimentation and the commitment to ecumenical openness and intellectual honesty.
